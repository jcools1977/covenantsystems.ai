1. Services

1.1 Scope of Work

Consultant agrees to provide the following services ("Services"):

(a) Automation Assessment: A structured review of Client's current workflows to identify automation opportunities, including client intake, document generation, case communications, and practice management processes.

(b) Custom Workflow Development: Design and implementation of two to three (2-3) custom automation workflows tailored to Client's practice, using industry-standard automation platforms (n8n, Make, Zapier, or similar).

(c) Integration: Integration of workflows with Client's existing tools and systems, which may include but are not limited to: practice management software (Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther), document management systems, email platforms (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365), e-signature services (DocuSign, HelloSign), and scheduling tools (Calendly).

(d) AI-Powered Features: Implementation of artificial intelligence capabilities for document customization, data extraction, and intelligent routing, subject to Client approval of specific use cases.

(e) Training and Documentation: Provision of user training materials, workflow documentation, and operation guides to enable Client's staff to use and maintain the delivered workflows.

(f) Post-Deployment Support: Thirty (30) days of support following workflow deployment to address technical issues, perform minor refinements, and answer usage questions.

1.2 Deliverables

Consultant will deliver:

  • Written automation assessment report identifying recommended workflows
  • Two to three (2-3) fully functional automated workflows deployed in Client's environment
  • User documentation and training materials
  • Access credentials and administrative rights to workflows (Client retains full control)

1.3 Timeline

Services will be performed over a period of approximately two to four (2-4) weeks from the Effective Date, subject to Client availability for review sessions and access to necessary systems. Specific milestones:

  • Week 1: Automation assessment and workflow design approval
  • Weeks 2-3: Development, testing, and integration
  • Week 4: Training, deployment, and post-deployment support period begins
